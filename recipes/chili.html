Chili Recipe

This is one of my favorite recipes for anytime of the year. I typically prepare it vegan but feel free to add a ground meat if you want. This dish is packed with protein and it fills you up. Don't feel the need to follow the recipe too closely. It's chili... it's hard to mess up. Don't overthink it.

Ingredients

  • Lots of different beans
  • Tomato Puree
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Zuchinni
  • Mushrooms
  • Serrano Pepper
  • Habanero Pepper
  • Canned Chipotle
  • Cumin
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Sautee onions, garlic, mushrooms, zuchinni, and peppers
  2. Add beans, chipotle, and tomato puree
  3. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper
  4. Stir frequently and taste
  5. Add seasoning accordingly
  6. If you want more spice, add another serrano or habenero
  7. Serve in a bowl with cream, cilantro, chopped onion, and tortillas or bread
