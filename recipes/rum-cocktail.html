Rum Cocktail

This is a simple rum cocktail. Feel free to use your white rum of choice. I'm broke so I just go with Bacardi. This is a good drink for a hot day. You can make it look fancier than it really is with a garnish or some extra flair.

Ingredients

  • White rum
  • Limes
  • Soda water
  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Throw in some ice
  2. Pour in your desired amount of alchohol
  3. Squeeze in two limes
  4. Throw in your sliced berries
  5. Pour in the soda water
  6. Stir and serve
